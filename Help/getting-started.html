Getting Started

Install, configure, and run your first research project

What is Beep.AI.Researcher?

Beep.AI.Researcher is an AI-powered research platform that helps you manage documents, conduct qualitative analysis, write reports, and synthesize evidence. It integrates with a separate Beep.AI.Server for LLM/AI capabilities including RAG search, chat, AI-assisted coding, and more.

The platform is designed for researchers, analysts, and teams who need to work with large volumes of documents and extract meaningful insights -- all while keeping data within your own infrastructure.

Key Features

  • RAG-Powered AI -- All AI responses are grounded in your uploaded documents
  • Qualitative Analysis -- NVivo-style hierarchical coding with AI suggestions
  • Multi-Language -- Full support for English, Arabic, French, and Spanish
  • Collaboration -- Project members, comments, and shared workspaces
  • Multi-Tenant -- Organization-level isolation with role-based access
  • Self-Hosted -- All data stays within your infrastructure
