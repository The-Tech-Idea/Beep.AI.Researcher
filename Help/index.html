Platform Overview

Beep.AI.Researcher is an advanced research platform that combines document management, AI-assisted analysis, and collaborative writing tools. It is designed for researchers, analysts, and teams who need to work with large volumes of documents and extract meaningful insights.

Documents

Upload, organize, and view PDFs, Word docs, and text files with AI-powered search.

Codes & Themes

Hierarchical qualitative coding with AI-suggested codes and reference tracking.

AI Chat

RAG-powered chat grounded in your project documents with quality modes.

Extraction

Define schemas and extract structured data from documents automatically.

Reports & Writing

Write reports with AI assistance, grammar checking, and citation management.

References

Manage citation libraries with Zotero sync and DOI validation.

Data & Charts

Upload CSV/XLSX data, chat with datasets, and generate visualizations.

Evidence Synthesis

AI-generated synthesis reports with evidence tables and contradiction analysis.

Training

AI-generated flashcards and quizzes from your project documents.

Tasks

Kanban-style task management with AI-suggested research tasks.

AI Discovery

Personalized research feed with paper recommendations and reading lists.

Export

Export data in JSON, CSV, Excel, SPSS, and compliance bundle formats.

Key Features

  • RAG-Powered AI — All AI responses are grounded in your uploaded documents
  • Qualitative Analysis — NVivo-style hierarchical coding with AI suggestions
  • Multi-Language — Full support for English, Arabic, French, and Spanish
  • Collaboration — Project members, comments, and shared workspaces
  • Multi-Tenant — Organization-level isolation with role-based access
  • Self-Hosted — All data stays within your infrastructure
  • MFA & SSO — Enterprise-grade authentication security
