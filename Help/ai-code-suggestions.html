AI Code Suggestions

Get AI-suggested codes for document passages

How It Works

When Beep.AI.Server is connected, the AI can analyze document passages and suggest relevant codes from your codebook.

Using Suggestions

  1. Open a document in the viewer
  2. The AI will analyze each chunk and suggest codes
  3. Review suggestions and accept or reject each one
  4. Accepted suggestions are applied as normal codes
